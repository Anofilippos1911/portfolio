Filippos Panteli

My name is Filippos Panteli. I am from Larnaca, Cyprus. I was born in 1994 and currently i am 26 years old. Μy love for computing started since i was 8 years old, from the very first time i was in front of my computer's screen, that was enough. Back then, if you gave me a keyboard, a mouse and a screen i felt like i was a superhero with endless possibilities and supernatural powers!

Eventually the years passed and my knowledge involving computers in general grew bigger and bigger. After some years and right before my adulthood i decided to drop the computing future and get involved in education, so that i could become a teacher in primary education. After successfully acquiring a place in university of Patras, Greece at 2014 i went there to study.

After 4 unforgettable, unremarkable and full of memories years i managed to get my degree and in 2019 i finished my studies there. In summer 2018 i reestablished back in my homecountry and after trying several jobs i got a part time job in teaching. I felt that this job wasnt for me, unless i teach computers.

Around the same time as i was back in Cyprus, i slowly but steadely rediscovered my love for computing. I enrolled in a couple of online courses through an online learning website and one thing led to another, i started buying more courses in different areas of Computing and Networking.

So i am here today, having taken the most important decision in my life as for now, to take a bachelor's degree in computing, as i wanted to do since i was a kid. So in Oct 2020 i will start a Computing and IT BSc in the Open University of UK.
