Hey there my name is Filippos Panteli. I am from Larnaca, Cyprus. I was born in 1994 and currently i am 26 years old. So my love for computing started since i was 8 years old, from the first time i was in front of my computer, I LOVED IT!! if you gave me a keyboard, a mouse and a screen it was like i was a NASA scientist! Eventually the years passed and my knowledge grew bigger and bigger.

As the years went by and i grew up i decided to to become a teacher in primary education. After successfully acquiring a place in university of Patras, Greece at 2014 i went there to study.And after 4 years and a half i managed to get my degree and in 2019 i finished my studies there.

Previously in summer 2018 i reestablished back in my homecountry and after trying several jobs i got a part time job in teaching. I felt that this job wasnt for me, unless i teach computers.

Around the same time as i was back in Cyprus, i slowly but steadely rediscovered my love for computing. I enrolled in a couple of online courses and thats it. In Oct 2020 i will start a Computing and IT bachelors degree in the Open University of UK.
